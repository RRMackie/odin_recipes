Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

Meat Sauce

  • 500g minced beef (or beef & pork mix)
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 x 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper

Cheese Filling

  • 250g ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 1 medium egg
  • 50g grated Parmesan

Other items:

  • 9–12 lasagne sheets (fresh or dried)
  • 200–250g grated mozzarella
  • Salt & pepper
  • Optional: Fresh basil or parsley

Method:

  1. Make the mince

    2. Fry mince in a pan until browned. Add onion and garlic; cook until soft. Stir in tomatoes, purée, herbs, salt & pepper. Simmer 15–20 mins.

  2. Prepare cheese filling

    3. Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt & pepper in a bowl.

  3. Cook pasta (if needed)

    4. Boil dried sheets as per packet. Fresh sheets can go straight in.

  4. Assemble layers

    5. In a baking dish:

    • Spoon of sauce
    • Lasagne sheets
    • Half cheese mix
    • Mozzarella
    • Repeat until layered
    • Finish with sauce + mozzarella on top
  5. Bake

    6. Cover with foil and bake at 190°C (fan 170°C) / Gas Mark 5 for 25 mins. Uncover and bake another 20–25 mins until golden.

  6. Rest & Serve

    7. Leave to stand for 10 - 15 mins before slicing
