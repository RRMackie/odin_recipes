Burger Recipe

Ingredients (Makes 2 burgers):

Beef Patties

  • 250g minced beef (ideally 15–20% fat)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard (optional)
  • Salt & pepper
  • A little oil for frying

For Assembly :

  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 slices cheddar or burger cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices red onion
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mayo or burger sauce

Method:

  1. Make the patties

    2. In a bowl, gently mix mince with mustard, salt & pepper (don’t overwork). Form into 2 equal-sized patties, slightly larger than the buns.

  2. Cook the patties

    3. Heat a frying pan or griddle over medium-high heat. Add a splash of oil, then cook patties for 3–4 mins each side, until browned and cooked through. Add cheese on top during the last minute and cover to melt.

  3. Toast the buns

    4. Slice buns and toast them cut-side down in a dry pan or under the grill until golden.

  4. Assemble

    5. Layer bottom bun with lettuce → patty with melted cheese → tomato → onion → pickles → sauce → top bun.

  5. Serve

    6. With chips, onion rings or a crisp salad.
