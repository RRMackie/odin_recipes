Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

Broth Base

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 750ml chicken or veg stock
  • 1 tbsp mirin (optional)
  • 1 tsp chilli oil or paste (optional)

Noodles and Toppings:

  • 2 portions ramen noodles (fresh or dried)
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs (6–7 mins boil)
  • 1 chicken breast or 150g tofu (pan-fried or grilled)
  • Handful of spinach or pak choi
  • 1 spring onion, finely sliced
  • Optional: sweetcorn, mushrooms, nori, sesame seeds

Method:

  1. Prepare the Broth

    2. In a pan, heat sesame oil. Add garlic & ginger, cook for 1 min. Stir in soy, miso paste, mirin, chilli (if using), then pour in stock. Simmer for 10–15 mins.

  2. Cook noodles

    3. Boil ramen noodles as per packet. Drain and set aside.

  3. Cook toppings

    4. Soft-boil eggs (6–7 mins), cool and peel. Cook chicken or tofu, season lightly. Wilt greens in hot broth or steam briefly.

  4. Assemble

    5. Divide noodles between bowls. Pour over hot broth. Top with sliced chicken/tofu, egg halves, greens, and extras like spring onion, sweetcorn or nori.

  5. Serve

    6. Sprinkle with sesame seeds or drizzle chilli oil for extra flavour
